The aim of this course is to foster and promote knowledge of complex value functions, Fourier transform, Laplace transform, the founder and solution of
mathematical and physics equations.The course covers the analysis of complex value functions, calculating some complex integrals with residues, character of
special functions, for example, Bessel function, associate Legendre function, and the special Bessel functions. It also discussed the some solution of partial
differential equation The teaching is performed using lectures, collaborative learning, and independent learning. The assessment is performed through an intermediate and a final written exam.
