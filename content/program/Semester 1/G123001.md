## Course overview

he aim of this course is to foster and promote knowledge of the major theoretical and practical issues of socialism with Chinese characteristics. The course covers the discussion of the latest achievements of the localization of Marxism. It also discusses the principles and measures to strengthen and innovate social governance. The teaching is performed using lectures, collaborative learning, and independent learning. The assessment is performed through a midterm exam and a final written exam.

## What you will learn

- Identify themes and basic issues of contemporary China
- Summarize the major theoretical and practical issues of socialism with Chinese characteristics
- Master the main content of the theoretical system of socialism with Chinese characteristics
- Analyze and solve practical problems by using this theory
- Identify and summarize common problems in society
